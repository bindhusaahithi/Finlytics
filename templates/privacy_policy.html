Privacy
Finlytics

This policy explains what Finlytics stores and how your account data is used.

Information we store

Finlytics stores your name, email address, encrypted password, transactions, budgets, debts, and debt payments so your dashboard can work correctly.

How your data is used

Your data is used only to provide personal finance tracking, summaries, charts, budget progress, and debt analytics inside your account.

Account deletion

You can delete your account from inside the app. Deleting your account permanently removes your saved financial data from Finlytics.

Security

Finlytics uses password hashing for account security. You should still use a strong password and avoid sharing your login details.

Contact and updates

Update this page with your support email before App Store submission so users and Apple reviewers can reach you if needed.
